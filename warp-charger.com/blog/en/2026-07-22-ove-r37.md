The WARP4 Charger has been successfully tested by TÜV AUSTRIA GMBH for conformity with the OVE guideline R 37.

Starting December 15, 2026, a test report according to OVE R 37 will be mandatory for charging equipment in Austria. The test report must be issued by a testing body accredited according to ÖVE/ÖNORM EN ISO/IEC 17025. With the certification by TÜV AUSTRIA GMBH, the WARP4 Charger already fulfills this requirement today.
